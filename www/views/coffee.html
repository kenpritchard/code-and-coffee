Chuckeyta's Cafe

411 Flagler Ave
New Smyrna Beach, FL 32169
P:(386) 428-2020

It's located in the building that used to house Nichols Surf Shop which just moved to a little building next door. There are surfboards and other related items in the eclectic decor and the beach is just a couple blocks away. Awesome atmosphere here.

Caffe Di Riverwalk

225 W Seminole Blvd #160
Sanford, FL 32771
P:(407) 287-5681

As the name implies, it's located on the St. John's river right on the Sanford Riverwalk trail. They've got really good food and coffee.

Black Point Cafe

882 North Point St
San Francisco, CA 94109
P:(415) 800-8448

Black Point is located nearly in the middle of the tourist district but has a distinct local feel and great coffee. The rustic wood timber on a steep hill creates a very interesting setting.
